# 排班模块Data层编译命令

## 编译整个schedule模块（推荐）
./gradlew :feature:schedule:compileDebugKotlin

## 清理并重新编译
./gradlew :feature:schedule:clean :feature:schedule:compileDebugKotlin

## 如果出现缓存问题，执行完整清理
./gradlew clean
rm -rf .gradle/
rm -rf feature/schedule/build/
./gradlew :feature:schedule:compileDebugKotlin

## 编译并查看详细错误
./gradlew :feature:schedule:compileDebugKotlin --stacktrace
