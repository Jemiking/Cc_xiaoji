# 排班模块集成编译命令

## 编译整个项目（推荐）
./gradlew clean build

## 分步编译验证

### 1. 编译feature:schedule模块
./gradlew :feature:schedule:compileDebugKotlin

### 2. 编译app模块
./gradlew :app:compileDebugKotlin

### 3. 运行Hilt注解处理
./gradlew :app:hiltJavaCompileDebug

## 安装测试
./gradlew installDebug

## 运行单元测试
./gradlew test

## 如果出现问题，清理并重建
./gradlew clean
rm -rf .gradle/
rm -rf app/build/
rm -rf feature/schedule/build/
./gradlew build
